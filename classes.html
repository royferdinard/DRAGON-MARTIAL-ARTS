Martial arts we train

Explore our programs for all ages and skill levels. Each martial art builds confidence, discipline, and self-defense skills.

Karate

Age group

Kids, Teens, Adults

Overview

Traditional Karate training emphasizing kihon (basics), kata (forms), kumite (sparring), and discipline.

Benefits

  • Improved focus and self-control
  • Increased strength, flexibility, and coordination
  • Confidence and leadership development
  • Stress relief and mental clarity
  • Respect for self and others

Training areas

  • Kihon – fundamental punches, kicks, and blocks
  • Kata – traditional forms and movement patterns
  • Kumite – controlled sparring and timing
  • Dojo Etiquette – respect, focus, and discipline
  • Belt Progression System

Taekwondo

Age group

Kids, Teens, Adults

Overview

High-energy Taekwondo focusing on kicking techniques, agility, flexibility, and controlled sparring.

Benefits

  • Improved flexibility, balance, and coordination
  • Increased speed and cardiovascular fitness
  • Confidence, discipline, and focus
  • Leadership development for youth
  • Stress relief and mental clarity

Training areas

  • Basic Techniques – punches, kicks, blocks, stances
  • Poomsae (Forms) – patterns to develop balance and precision
  • Sparring – controlled Olympic-style training
  • Flexibility & Conditioning
  • Belt Progression System & Testing

Kickboxing

Age group

Kids, Teens, Adults

Overview

Dynamic striking-focused program combining punches and kicks for fitness and self-defense..

Benefits

  • Improved strength, endurance, and coordination
  • High-calorie burn and weight management
  • Stress relief and mental toughness
  • Increased confidence and discipline

Training areas

  • Proper stance, footwork, and movement
  • Punching and kicking combinations
  • Pad work and heavy bag training
  • Conditioning and cardio drills
  • Optional controlled sparring

Boxing

Age group

Kids, Teens, Adults

Overview

Our Boxing program delivers authentic, high-quality training focused on proper technique, conditioning, and ring awareness. Classes are structured to improve fitness while developing real boxing skills.

Benefits

  • Increased strength and cardiovascular fitness
  • Improved coordination and reflexes
  • Stress relief and mental toughness
  • Confidence and discipline

Trainning areas

  • Stance, footwork, and movement
  • Punching combinations & defense
  • Pad work and heavy bag training
  • Conditioning & endurance
  • Controlled sparring (optional)

Kung' fu

Age group

Kids, Teens, Adults

Overview

Our Kung Fu program teaches traditional techniques, forms, and conditioning designed to develop physical strength, coordination, and mental discipline. Training emphasizes control, precision, and respect for martial arts tradition.

Benefits

  • Improved balance, flexibility, and coordination
  • Enhanced focus and mental discipline
  • Strength and body control
  • Confidence and discipline
  • Stress reduction and inner calm

Trainning areas

  • Fundamental stances, strikes, and blocks
  • Traditional forms (Taolu)
  • Balance, flexibility, and body conditioning
  • Breathing, focus, and mental control
  • Self-defense applications

Wing' chun

Age group

Kids, Teens, Adults

Overview

Our Wing Chun program teaches a systematic approach to self-defense, emphasizing efficiency, economy of motion, and control. Training develops strong structure, sensitivity, and practical application for real-world situations.

Benefits

  • Effective close-range self-defense
  • Improved coordination and reflexes
  • Enhanced focus and body awareness
  • Confidence without relying on strength
  • Mental discipline and control

Trainning areas

  • Centerline Theory & Structure
  • Hand Techniques & Footwork
  • Chi Sao (Sticking Hands)
  • Forms Training (Siu Nim Tao, Chum Kiu, Biu Jee)
  • Self-Defense Applications
